Mindfulness

Mindfulness is the act of being intentionally aware of the present moment and observing our thoughts, emotions and physical sensations without any judgement.

Mindfulness practice is the profound experience of cultivating curiosity and exploring how we habitually respond to everyday challenges in life.

Thich Nhat Hanh

Mindfulness is what brings us back in touch with what’s happening in the present moment in our body, in our feelings, in our thinking, and also in our environment.
Compassion

Compassion is the feeling that arises while recognizing and empathizing with the pain of another person, along with the wish and effort to alleviate suffering.

Dalai Lama

The wish that all sentient beings may be free from suffering.
Self-Compassion

Self-Compassion is the act of being kind to oneself in the midst of suffering. Simply put it is compassion directed toward oneself.

Mindful Self-Compassion practice is the profound experience of actively comforting, protecting and supporting ourselves when we’re in pain, by using resources within, recalibrating our default mode.

Rehabilitation Homes

Rehabilitation Homes

RLHP has estaiblished two shelter homes in 1999 in Mysore- Innam uttantahalli - Ashakirana (Ray of Hope) for boys and Ashabhavana-Srirampura (House of Hope) for girls - to provide safe environment to children who are orphaned, malnourished and estranged due to poverty, domestic violence, trafficking, sexual abuse, etc.
-Integrated rehabilitation care is given to the children by providing food, shelter, clothing, healthcare, value education, counselling support and life skills. The children get the opportunity to actively engage in sports, yoga, meditation, computer usage and cultural

events and develop their inner competencies and creativity. They also engage in crucial discussions related to social and environmental issues.
-Children at shelter homes are enrolled into government schools and engaged in various experiential workshops and summer camps. Furthermore, children are supported and encouraged to be self-reliant and pursue higher studies after 12th grade. RLHP supports them by providing vocational training support in specialised courses like Engineering, Law, Nursing and Lab Technician.

Eradication of Child Labour

Eradication of Child Labour

Children are forced to work in harsh conditions and instead of attending school, often end up with detrimental physical and mental health problems such as depression, premature ageing and drug addictions. -RLHP advocates for the basic human rights of those children by organizing awareness camps on child rights and CLRPA, so that the street and migrant children can be prevented from being trapped in the clutches child labour. -RLHP liaises with like-minded organisations, various state and district level stakeholders and supports them in preparing intensive action plans to confront and prohibit child labour within their operational areas.

Childline

Childline 1098

CHILDLINE 1098 responds to emergency needs of children in crisis including child labourers, street children and victims of trafficking, exploitation, substance abusers, child marriage, etc. -With the support of the Childline India Foundation, Ministry of Women and Child Development and other NGOs, the Child Collab Center at RLHP has been running for the past 10 years and ensuring 24x7 immediate safety and rehabilitation services to children in crises. -RLHP organises street plays, cycle rallies, distributes handbills and erects stalls in schools and public places to sensitise people about the significance of the 24/7 toll free helpline service of CHILDLINE 1098.

Quality education for children
